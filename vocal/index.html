My Vocabulary Notebook

Ephemeral

adjective
/ɪˈfem.ər.əl/
Phù du, chóng tàn
Definition

Lasting for a very short time; transient or fleeting.

Example

"Fame in the age of the internet is often ephemeral, vanishing as quickly as it appears."

Meticulous

adjective
/məˈtɪk.jə.ləs/
Tỉ mỉ, kỹ càng
Definition

Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.

Example

"Many hours of meticulous preparation have gone into making the web application seamless."

Ameliorate

verb
/əˈmiːl.ju.reɪt/
Cải thiện, làm tốt hơn
Definition

To make something bad or unsatisfactory better.

Example

"The new reform was introduced to ameliorate the living conditions of the citizens."
